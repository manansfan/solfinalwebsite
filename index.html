Summer of Learning?

Summer of Learning Program - CCEW is a student initiative to encourage effective online learning through massive open online courses (MOOCs) via a mentor-mentee system, with the secondary aim to create a healthy academic peer culture among CCEW students.

Mentor Responsibilities:

Resolve mentee’s doubts regarding the course as best as you can. Revise and Master a subject!

Mentee Responsibilities:

Select 4 courses from the course list in your order of preference, during the Mentee Sign-up phase. Ensure that you will complete the course in the estimated time. Follow the course timelines diligently to get the most out of SOL Produce a detailed report (minimum 10 pages) within 1 week of course completion
